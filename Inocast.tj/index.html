News

26-27 March 2015 the second project workshop passed in Astana (Kazakhstan). Vice Rector for International Relations Abdurahim Juraev and project manager Jamshedkhon Bakhovudinov participated in it.
During the seminar issues of development of innovative laboratories at universities, methods of management and strategic problems of the organization of their activities were considered.

In particular, the following issues were highlighted:


- the possibility of co-operation between scientists and entrepreneurs;
- the most promising types of services for the initial phase of laboratory activities;
- planning of further activities of innovative laboratories.

From 19 June until 12 July 2015 Abdurahim Juraev as a project coordinator and Jamshedkhon Bakhovudinov as the project manager took part in a study visit to European innovative laboratories. During the visit they visited an innovative laboratory of Boras University (Sweden), of Autonomous University of Barcelona (Spain) and of Coventry University (UK) and got acquainted with their activities.

2-5 November 2015 regular seminar of the project was held in Almaty (Kazakhstan) at the KIMEP University. Project coordinator Abdurahim Juraev and project manager Alisher Kurbonaliev participated in it. Abdurahim Juraev made a presentation on the topic "Perspective Plan of the TSULBP Innovative Laboratory." The participants visited Innovative Technopark "Alatau" and got acquainted with the features of its activities.

9-11 March 2016 the next seminar of the project will take place at the Bukhara State University (Uzbekistan).
